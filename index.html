about

Kasia Pluto (b. 1995) is an American artist and art researcher based in Reading, Pennsylvania, primarily working in drawing and painting. She graduated with her BFA in Painting & Drawing with a minor in Art History from Tyler School of Art, Temple University in 2017, and graduated with her MA in Design Research from BAU College of Arts and Design Barcelona in 2024. She finds artistic influence in nature, tech/tinker culture, and her daily experience.
