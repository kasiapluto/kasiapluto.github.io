about

Kasia Pluto (b. 1995) is a Polish-American artist and art researcher based in Pennsylvania; primarily working in drawing and painting. She graduated with her BFA in Painting & Drawing with a minor in Art History from Tyler School of Art, Temple University in 2017, and graduated with her MA in Design Research from BAU College of Arts and Design Barcelona in 2024. She finds artistic influence in nature, tech/tinker culture, and her daily experience.

artist statement

I work at the intersection of oil painting, technoethics, and hyperculture. I'm highly inspired by technology I use on a daily basis as well as my lived experience. I'm fascinated with oil painting - for it's existing history, the living history, ecological way of making, and the use of an ancient object as a daily ritual I help maintain in modernity. My favorite mediums are oil paint, charcoal, ink, and coding. My fave color is pink! I like chartreuse too.
